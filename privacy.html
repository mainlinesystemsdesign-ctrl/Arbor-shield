Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

Mainline Systems ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the ArborShield platform (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

To provide professional documentation services for the tree care industry, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Name, business email, phone number, and billing information (processed securely via Stripe).
  • Field Data: Images and videos uploaded for site documentation, as well as GPS location data used to mark tree assets or technician locations.
  • Usage Data: Information on how the Service is accessed, including device type and log data, to ensure platform stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ArborShield platform.
  • Process subscription payments and manage account billing.
  • Generate project reports and documentation for your clients.
  • Communicate technical updates or administrative notices.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Mainline Systems does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted partners (e.g., Stripe for payments, Google Cloud for hosting) who assist in operating our Service.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. User Rights (Your Choices)

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Portability: You may request a copy of the field data associated with your account.
  • Correction & Deletion: You may update your account information at any time. You may request the deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to certain legal or contractual retention requirements.
  • Camera and Location Access: You may opt-out of GPS or camera access via your device settings, though this will significantly limit the Service’s functionality.

5. Our Rights and Data Retention

Mainline Systems reserves the right to retain anonymized, de-identified data for the purposes of improving our internal algorithms and platform performance. We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to use industry-standard protections.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
